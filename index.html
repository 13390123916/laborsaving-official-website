About LABOR-SAVING

Leading manufacturer of ergonomic lift assistance devices and pneumatic manipulator solutions

Revolutionizing Industrial Handling Since Day One

LABOR-SAVING is a premier manufacturer specializing in advanced pneumatic manipulators and zero-gravity balance arms. Our mission is to revolutionize workplace ergonomics by providing innovative lifting solutions that protect workers while maximizing operational efficiency.

Located in Shenyang, China, our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge engineering with precision manufacturing to deliver products that meet international quality standards. Each unit is meticulously crafted to ensure reliability, safety, and outstanding performance.
